Mystical Rock Music by Victor Fisher#

Reenchantment is a rock and metal solo project by Victor Fisher. The name originates from Max Weber’s theory of disenchantment. The goal of the project is to reenchant the world through the use of music or other spiritual practices. Please visit the links page for more information.
